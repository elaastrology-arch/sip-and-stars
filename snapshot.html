Your Free Mini Sun-Sign Snapshot

A sip-sized read on the sign the sun was wearing the day you were born.

You're in, and here's your first sip: a quick read on your Sun sign, the part of your chart everyone mentions and almost nobody explains properly. It's not your whole story (that's what your Moon, your Rising, and the rest of your sky are for), but it's a good place to start.

Aries · Mar 21 – Apr 19

You lead with instinct, not overthinking, and that's rarer than it sounds. Your gift is momentum: the courage to start what everyone else is still deciding on. The practice: let one thing stay unfinished without calling it failure.

Taurus · Apr 20 – May 20

You build slow and you build to last; comfort isn't a weakness, it's your whole strategy. Your gift is steadiness: the people around you borrow your calm more than you realize. The practice: let something change before you feel fully ready for it.

Gemini · May 21 – Jun 20

Your mind runs on curiosity, and you collect ideas the way other people collect souvenirs. Your gift is connection: turning a stray thought into the exact words someone else needed to hear. The practice: pick one conversation to actually finish instead of chasing the next one.

Cancer · Jun 21 – Jul 22

You feel first and think second, and your memory for how people made you feel is nearly perfect. Your gift is care: you build the kind of safety most people spend years looking for. The practice: let yourself receive the tending you're so good at giving.

Leo · Jul 23 – Aug 22

You were built to be seen, and pretending otherwise has never suited you. Your gift is warmth: a room feels different once you walk into it. The practice: let someone else hold the spotlight and notice you don't disappear.

Virgo · Aug 23 – Sep 22

You notice the detail everyone else missed, and you quietly fix it before anyone says thank you. Your gift is devotion: you love through action, not declarations. The practice: let something stay imperfect and call it done anyway.

Libra · Sep 23 – Oct 22

You read a room before you say a word, and fairness matters to you more than winning. Your gift is harmony: you make people feel like they're finally being heard. The practice: pick a side, even when it's uncomfortable.

Scorpio · Oct 23 – Nov 21

You go straight for what's real and skip the small talk if you can help it. Your gift is depth: you're the person people trust with the version of themselves they don't show anyone else. The practice: let go of one thing you've been quietly gripping.

Sagittarius · Nov 22 – Dec 21

You're chasing the next horizon before you've unpacked from the last trip. Your gift is perspective: you make people feel bigger than their smallest problem. The practice: let one plan stay unplanned and see what happens.

Capricorn · Dec 22 – Jan 19

You were building a plan while everyone else was still finding the syllabus. Your gift is follow-through: you turn ideas other people abandon into something real. The practice: let rest count as progress too.

Aquarius · Jan 20 – Feb 18

You've never quite fit the mold, and honestly, you stopped trying to a while ago. Your gift is vision: you see the version of things nobody else has imagined yet. The practice: let one person get close enough to actually know you.

Pisces · Feb 19 – Mar 20

You feel the room before you understand why; your intuition rarely needs proof to be right. Your gift is imagination: you turn feeling into something others can finally see. The practice: build one boundary and let it hold.
